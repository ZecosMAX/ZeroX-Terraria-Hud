This is a mod that shows you different kinds of in-game statistics for your party.
Very useful if you want to feel like healer from good ol' [MMORPG name here].

This mod is still in development, so lots of new differents stats are coming as well as settings for your personal customisation!

Github page: https://github.com/ZecosMAX/ZeroX-Terraria-Hud
Current version: v0.2.2

As for available stats
Red bar is showing your current health and regen.
Blue bar is showing your current mana and regen.

DEF - defence
DPS - damage per second.
AP - Armor piercing
Speed - speed multiplier, affected by buffs and de-buffs.
Magic/Melee/Range/Summon - Damage total multiplier, both additive and multiplicative in one number
